**Author:** WaveHeart.澜心
**Title:** 裸辞VP的一人公司，拆解可复制思路 (Deconstructing a Resigned VP's One-Person Company: Replicable Strategy)

## Subject

The post analyzes **Justin Welsh** — a former senior VP at a large tech company who left due to burnout and built a solo operation generating **$5M+ annually with 90%+ profit margins**.

## Three core steps to replicate

1. **Establish professional credibility** (建立专业标签)
2. **Create standardized products** (做标准化产品)
3. **Build automated systems** (搭自动化系统)

## Stated principle

The author emphasizes **"leverage"** as the key principle: it allows one person to reach global markets without hiring employees.
