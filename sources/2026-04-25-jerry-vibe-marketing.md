## Concept introduced

**Vibe Marketing** — an emerging concept combining **cultural intuition + creative instinct + AI-driven automation**, enabling individual marketers to scale emotionally resonant campaigns.

## Core philosophy

Marketing/business leader plays the role of **director** — strategizing and steering AI tools and workflows. This replaces traditional entry-level positions, agency partners, and interns. The result is a **"super individual + AI" marketing system** ("超级个体 + AI").

## Four implementation stages

| Stage | Activity |
|---|---|
| 1. Foundation | Establish brand voice and audience targeting |
| 2. Directives | Develop directives via social listening to identify marketing angles |
| 3. Content | AI drafts → humans refine and iterate |
| 4. Distribution | Distribute across channels, optimize using real-time data |
