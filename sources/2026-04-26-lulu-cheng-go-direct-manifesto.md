# Key extracted facts

## Three-part argument

1. **Traditional PR is obsolete** — media gatekeepers and third-party publicists are no longer necessary given direct social/email access to audiences.

2. **Founders must own communications** — "The best spokesperson for any endeavor is… the person who holds the secret knowledge." Cannot be outsourced to hired representatives.

3. **Direct narrative building** — founders should "craft and tell your own story, without being dependent on intermediaries" to maintain control and competitive advantage.

## Core principle

Direct founder communication creates authenticity and movement-building that outsourced messaging cannot replicate.

## Reference ("Rostra")

Lulu's company is named Rostra, after the speaker's platform in ancient Rome — metaphor for direct speech to your audience without intermediaries.
