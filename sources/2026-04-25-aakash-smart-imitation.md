## Core thesis

Robinhood's $100B+ valuation was not driven by the no-commission trading model itself, but by the **deeper product strategy** beneath it. The lesson: don't imitate the surface feature, imitate the underlying product logic.

> 文章揭示Robinhood突破千亿美元估值的真实驱动力并非免佣金交易模式本身，而是深层的产品策略。

## The four principles

1. **Identify the user's core psychological need** — go beyond surface features and dig into the user's real motivation.
2. **Position AI as an information tool, not a decision replacement** — preserve the user's autonomy in decision-making.
3. **Productize the compliance function** — convert the compliance team from an obstacle into a product partner; integrate regulatory requirements into UX.
4. **Polish the experience to an extreme** — optimize every touchpoint of the user interaction.

## Scope claim

These principles are framed as transferable beyond fintech — applicable across industries as general product wisdom for PMs.
