## What it is

**marketingskills** — a library of 25 marketing-specific AI skills created by **Corey Haines** (founder of Conversion Factory). Released under the MIT license as free software.

## How it operates

Before invocation, the user fills in a **product profile questionnaire**:

- Product definition
- Problem the product solves
- Target audience
- Competitors
- Differentiation
- Brand tone
- Pricing strategy

The AI then executes its skills against this filled-in profile — outputs are customized to the specific product context rather than generic.

## Coverage

The 25 skills span:

- CRO (conversion-rate optimization)
- Copywriting
- SEO
- Email marketing
- Growth
- Advertising
- Customer retention

## Author's recommendation rationale

> 你填的越详细，后面输出的越准
> The more detailed you fill out the profile, the more precise the output.

Users can pick which skills to deploy based on current need — no requirement to run all 25.
