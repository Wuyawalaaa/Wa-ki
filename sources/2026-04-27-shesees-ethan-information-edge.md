# 伊森：把信息差变成机会

**Author:** shesees阅读社区
**Title:** 伊森：把信息差变成机会 (Ethan: Turning Information Gaps into Opportunities)

## Subject

The post discusses **why some writers produce superior content** — arguing the advantage lies not in writing skill but in **information access**.

## Source attribution

References **Ethan, a former Morning Brew employee**, who shares methodology for uncovering exclusive stories.

## Core methodology

- Identify **"backstage figures with deep insights yet limited attention"** rather than chasing public information.
- Use **LinkedIn to create "talent maps"** identifying crucial roles in company growth.
- Employ this question repeatedly:

  > **"Who else should I speak with about this topic?"**

## Stated frame

The post frames this as more than a networking tactic — **it represents a mindset for accessing scarce information**.

## Closing

The post promotes a membership program offering curated content delivered daily, weekly, and monthly.
