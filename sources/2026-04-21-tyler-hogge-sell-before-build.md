# Tyler Hogge's "Sell Before Build" Validation Method

**Original URL:** http://xhslink.com/o/4Eefn8Bckk4
**Platform:** Xiaohongshu (小红书)
**Fetched:** 2026-04-21

## Core strategy

Counter-intuitive approach: **validate product demand through 30 real sales conversations using Figma prototypes before engineering development begins.**

## 7-step framework

1. Establish unique insights
2. Create Figma visual prototypes
3. Schedule 30 sales conversations
4. Make direct purchase requests
5. Iterate and optimize based on feedback
6. Repeat until achieving 30% conversion rate
7. Market validation confirms product-market fit

## Success metric

> 达到30%转化率就说明找到了市场迫切感。
> A 30% conversion rate indicates you've found genuine market urgency.

## Real-world examples

- **Flexport** — used a fake website to get major companies (including Foxconn) to register, validating demand before building freight-forwarding software.
- **Redo** — validated demand with a $100 waitlist approach.

## Core argument

> 初创公司最大的风险不是缺钱，而是妳花半年造出来的东西没人要。
> A startup's greatest risk isn't lack of funding, but building something for six months nobody wants.
