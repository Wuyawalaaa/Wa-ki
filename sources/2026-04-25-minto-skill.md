## What it is

**/Minto This** — a content-structure skill derived from Barbara Minto's Pyramid Principle (originally a McKinsey-era management-consulting communication framework). Adapted for social-media content optimization by Ole Lehmann.

## The two-step mechanism

1. **Lead with conclusion** — state your main point in a single sentence.
2. **Support with reasoning** — explain why; provide evidence and examples.

## What the structure forces

- "立刻进入核心观点，避免绕半天还没说重点" — get to the core point immediately, no preamble.
- Establish a clear position rather than generalized commentary.
- Supply substantive evidence rather than unsupported claims.

## MECE integration

The skill incorporates Barbara Minto's **MECE** principle — Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive — meaning supporting points must be non-overlapping and comprehensively address the thesis without redundancy.

Ole Lehmann reportedly produces 10M+ monthly impressions and $5,000+/month income on X using this method consistently.
