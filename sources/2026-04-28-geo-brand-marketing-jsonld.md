## 3-step implementation framework

**Step 1: Product page restructuring**
Transform product descriptions from transactional copy to pain-point resources. Example prompt used: "List 15 questions mothers worry about when selecting formula, then answer each using verified nutritional data and real feeding scenarios." Goal: pages AI systems want to reference, not just pages humans want to buy from.

**Step 2: Technical SEO with JSON-LD markup**
- Topic blogs answering common user questions
- Direct answers placed prominently (AI prioritizes this)
- FAQ schema + comparison tables
- All information encoded as structured data for AI parsing

**Step 3: Third-party credibility building**
Build brand mentions across authoritative platforms:
- Community forums: Reddit, Quora
- Guest posts + review features
- Review platforms: G2, Capterra, Product Hunt
- Press releases at product milestones
