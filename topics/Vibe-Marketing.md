# Vibe Marketing

> An emerging marketing posture where a single individual orchestrates AI tools and workflows like a director, replacing the traditional team-of-juniors-plus-agency stack. Combines cultural intuition + creative instinct + AI-driven automation.

## Definition

【事实】Vibe Marketing combines **cultural intuition + creative instinct + AI-driven automation**, enabling individual marketers to scale emotionally resonant campaigns. [JV]

## Core philosophy

【事实】The marketer (or business leader) plays the role of **director** — strategizing and steering AI tools and workflows. This replaces traditional entry-level positions, agency partners, and interns. The stated result is a "super individual + AI" marketing system (超级个体 + AI). [JV]

## Four-stage workflow

【事实】[JV]

| Stage | Activity |
|---|---|
| 1. Foundation | Establish brand voice and audience targeting |
| 2. Directives | Develop directives via social listening to identify marketing angles |
| 3. Content | AI drafts → humans refine and iterate |
| 4. Distribution | Distribute across channels, optimize using real-time data |

## Why this is more than a re-skin

【推断】Vibe Marketing is structurally distinct from "use AI to write copy." The shift is in **org shape**: traditional marketing assumed a team where the senior provides direction and juniors/agencies execute. AI collapses execution capacity into the senior, so the failure mode flips — execution is no longer the bottleneck; **direction quality** is. That's why all four stages front-load taste decisions (voice, audience, angles, distribution channels) and treat content generation as the cheapest step. A senior marketer with weak taste produces more bad output faster than before; a senior marketer with strong taste finally has the throughput to express it.

【推断】The director metaphor is load-bearing — directors don't write every line of dialogue, but every line passes through their judgment. Vibe Marketing only works if the human is genuinely directing (rejecting outputs, calibrating tone, killing weak angles), not rubber-stamping AI defaults. Without that, the four stages become four flavors of slop.
