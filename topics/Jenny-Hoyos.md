# Jenny Hoyos

> Short-form content creator cited for an "audience-first" viral content methodology. The post that triggered this entry frames her work as the underlying logic of traffic, not just a tactical playbook.

## Core principle (Hoyos-attributed reframe)

【事实】"传播的关键不是'我想表达什么'，而是'用户能接受什么'" — the key to going viral is not "what I want to express," but "what the audience can absorb." Audience receptivity is treated as upstream of self-expression. [JH]

## Why this matters

【推断】The "audience-first" framing is the substantive part of the entry. It is structurally the same move as Nielsen's [[Articulation-Barrier]] insight applied to short-form video: *the creator's job is not to express well, it is to be receivable*. The three capabilities (simplify, turn, transfer emotion) are mechanisms for receivability.
