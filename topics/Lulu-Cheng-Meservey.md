# Lulu Cheng Meservey

> Founder-led comms strategist. Former VP Comms at Substack, EVP Corporate Affairs at Activision Blizzard. Founder of Rostra, the advisory firm focused on founder-led communications.

## Why she matters

【推断】Lulu's body of work is the canonical practitioner reference for founders deciding how to handle their own communications without traditional PR. Her endorsements — Sam Altman (OpenAI), Brian Armstrong (Coinbase), Bari Weiss (The Free Press) — span tech, crypto, and media. The frameworks she has named have become the working vocabulary for a generation of founder-led marketing. [LM]

## Top-level structure of her doctrine

【事实】Four pillars of excellent communications: **Strategy / Tactics / Message / Delivery**. Three components within strategy: **Message / Medium / Messenger**. Underlying frame borrowed from military strategy: **Ends / Ways / Means**. [LM]

## How the frameworks compose

【推断】Reading from broadest to most tactical:

```
Go Direct (doctrine: speak directly to audience, not through PR)
    ↓
Concentric Circles (targeting: smallest passionate ring first, expand outward)
    ↓
Cultural Erogenous Zones (messaging: fit message to existing audience care-zones)
    ↓
Physics-Based Communications (force allocation: spread vs concentrate)
    ↓
Sticky Phrases (tactics: how to make the actual wording memorable)
```

Each framework operates at a different layer of abstraction. They're complementary, not competing — a complete comms move uses all five.
